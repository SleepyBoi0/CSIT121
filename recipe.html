Hershey's Chocolate Overloaded Cookies

The most delicious chocolate cookies ever

First, you'll need to gather the ingredients.

  • 1 Package of Devil's food chocolate cake mix
  • 3/4 Cup of butter, softened
  • 1/4 Cup of Hershey's Cocoa
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 Cups of Hershey's Milk Chocolate Chips

After gathering all of the ingredients, you're ready to get to baking.

  1. Heat oven to 350Â°F
  2. Mix all ingredients in large mixing bowl, adding in the Hershey's Chocolate chips last
  3. Roll dough into 2-inch balls and then place onto an ungreased cookie sheet
  4. Bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes
  5. Take out of the oven and set aside to cool
  6. Enjoy!

My mom first made these cookies when I was a kid and I absolutely loved them. They are the perfect amount of chocolate, I don't like things that are too chocolatey. These cookies are also the perfect consistency, I like my cookies soft and still a little gooy on the inside and if you make them right, these do exactly that.
